Objective

Abebi Adebisi

Obokun

" To provide at affordable cost effective integrated health care service designed to secure improvement in the physical, mental and socio-economic well being of the people of Obokun through Primary, Secondary and Tertiary health care services offered in a vertical combination structure."

Grace Ajibade

Obokun

"To provide an enabling environment for teaching, learning and research, while impacting professional skills and knowledge, behaviour and attitude that should contribute to advancing the health and productivity of the community without bias to gender, culture or creed."
